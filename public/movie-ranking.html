The Best Movies According to Nischitha

My top 3 movies of all-time.

Amrithadhare

I absolutely love Amrithadhare because it beautifully captures the essence of love and relationships in a way that feels both real and relatable. The characters of Puru and Amrutha, portrayed by Dhyan and Ramya, reflect the struggles and joys of marriage, which makes their story incredibly engaging. Their chemistry brings warmth to the screen, making you feel deeply connected to their journey.

The Kerala Story

I really appreciate Kerala Story for its bold storytelling and the way it sheds light on sensitive issues like manipulation and the complexities of faith. Adah Sharma's portrayal of Shalini is powerful and moving, capturing the emotional turmoil of her character. The film doesn't shy away from difficult subjects, which makes it a thought-provoking watch. It challenges viewers to confront uncomfortable truths about society, leaving a lasting impression and sparking important conversations.
